The OOPS Story

Established since 1995, Object Oriented Programming Services (OOPS) Pvt. Ltd. is a leading expert in Network Telephony applications.

Since inception, OOPS has been associated with the Indian Institute of Technology ( IIT ) Madras, India. The Department of Electrical Engineering, IIT Madras, has been the guiding force behind OOPS research team. Along with a few other like-minded organisations, OOPS is a member of the Telecommunications and Computer Networks Group - TeNeT - at IIT Madras.

We are developing a broad range of audio and video telecommunication products and offer them over the internet.

We provide a reliable, cost-effective software development infrastructure to clients in the international marketplace. We specialise in the development of networking and telecommunication applications.

Our main focus is to provide easy-to-use and efficient solutions for the networking and communications industry.

Our motto: Keep it simple . Make it Work
